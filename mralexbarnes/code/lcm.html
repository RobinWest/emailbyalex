Lavazza– Italy’s favourite coffee now available for cappuccino lovers everywhere!

Here at London Coffee Machines Ltd we are pleased to announce that we can now offer our customers the fresh ground bean to cup coffee experience sourced direct from Lavazza – Italy’s most famous coffee producers world wide.

Lavazza was founded by Luigi Lavazza in 1895 who began creating and roasting his own coffee blends as a small family business. For over a century now Lavazza’s dedication to the quality of beans used, production methods and continuous research & development has been core to the Lavazza brand’s success in producing among the world’s finest quality coffees.

For those of you who already have a traditional bean to cup coffee machine or are considering one for your office (tabletop or freestanding) we can now supply Lavazza Super Crema in 6 x 1kg cases – guaranteed to produce the highest quality cappuccinos, lattes and espresso at the touch of a button. For those of you that need a system that is a little more simple to maintain and perhaps don’t have access to a plumbed water supply Lavazza is also available in a capsule system called Espresso Point.

Each capsule contains real fresh ground coffee and is able to produce an excellent barista style cappuccino, latte or espresso – all of this with the minimum of fussy maintenance!

The N&W Easy system has been designed specifically around the Lavazza Espresso Point cartridge and allows a customer to enjoy a lovely frothy Lavazza cappuccino/latte (normal or decaffeinated) along with indulgent chocolate & espresso at the touch of a button. This tidy, compact tabletop machine doesn’t need to be plumbed, is comfortable on small counter tops or coffee tables and doesn’t require intensive cleaning procedures –hence the name “Easy”!

For more information on either Lavazza Super Crema beans or Lavazza Espresso Point capsules as well as the coffee machines themselves just give us a call on 0208 1111122 or email info@londoncoffeemachines.co.uk and we will be very happy to help!
